Political Democracy: Structural Equation Model

Description:

This data set, "Political Democracy", provides indicators of industrialization in 1960, and political democracy indicators in 1960 and 1965.

Variables:

  • x1, ... x3 - Indicators of industrialization in 1960.
  • y1, ... y4 - Indicators of political democracy in 1960.
  • y5, ... y8 - Indicators of political democracy in 1965.

This example JASP file demonstrates the use of structural equation modeling. Specifically, our model assumes three latent variables: Industrialization in 1960, Political Democracy in 1960, and Political Democracy in 1965. We test whether Political Democracy in 1960 and 1965 is related to Industrialization, and that Political Democracy in 1960 predicts Political Democracy in 1965.

For an original example of this analysis can be found at http://lavaan.ugent.be/tutorial/sem.html

Reference:

Bollen (1979). Political Democracy and the Timing of Development. American Sociological Review, 44: 572:587.
