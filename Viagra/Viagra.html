ANOVA

For illustrative purpose, let's run ANOVA testing differences in average libido between the groups (that is, without a covariate). We don't find a statistically significant effect at the .05 level.

ANCOVA

So what happens when the variance associated with the partner's Libido is accounted for? Suddenly the effect of dose becomes statistically significant at the .05 level (i.e., p = 0.027).
